Conversation Cafe IN PERSON: Calgary North

Are you living with dementia? Does your partner have dementia? Please join us! Conversation Cafe’s are open to anyone regardless of where you are in the dementia journey.

The purpose of Conversation Cafe is to bring people together for conversation and to support each other in this journey with dementia.

All Conversation Cafes have a host and often a co-host to facilitate conversation. We are a safe place for discussion, laughs, and friendship. We firmly believe in confidentiality.

For more information, please contact Alan at alanrae@telusplanet.net or Chelsea at chelsea.Jani@longevitycare.ca.
